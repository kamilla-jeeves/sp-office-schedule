Why this works for the team
🪑
No more uncertainty
Everyone knows exactly when they go in and their seat is guaranteed. No more wondering if there'll be space — or finding visitors in your spot.
🌎
Aligned with Colombia & Mexico
2 days in the office is already the standard for our other offices. Brazil aligns with the same rhythm — one consistent culture across the region.
📈
Easier to hire & grow
The rotation builds in room for new hires. As the team grows, we still have space — and the structure is easy to adjust without starting from scratch.
